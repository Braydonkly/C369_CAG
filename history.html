How it works

  1. Switch between different months (e.g., April, May, June, July, August) to view your transaction history for each specific month.
  2. Each transaction is listed with the date, vendor, items purchased or the nature of the transaction, and the transaction amount, providing a comprehensive overview of spending and top-ups.
